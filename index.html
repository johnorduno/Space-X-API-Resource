What kind of information can I find on here?

This is a project which uses the Space-X API to provide real-time data about Space-X flights and launches. This information includes flight patch images, mission details, video, links, and other misc. information regarding the flight. Click the links on the navigation bar at the top of the screen to view the type of flight information you are interested in.


WARNING:

Some of the flight details contain Space-X's technical jargon. Additional research may need to be conducted to fully understand their meaning.
